Why Malaysia

The numbers stack up. The lifestyle seals it.

Malaysia is one of the few places where Australians can genuinely upgrade their lifestyle while cutting their cost of living. Here is why thousands are looking north.

COST

Your dollar goes further

Your dollar goes further

Rent, dining, healthcare and help around the home cost a fraction of Australian prices. Some families may reduce everyday living costs substantially, depending on lifestyle, location and household needs.

PROPERTY

Freehold, high spec, attainable

Tap or hover to reveal

Freehold, high spec, attainable

Foreigners can generally purchase certain types of Malaysian property, subject to state rules, minimum thresholds and legal advice. Some freehold options may be available. Penang offers condominium options at price points far below many Australian capital-city markets, subject to location, exchange rates and current stock.

VISA

Clear residency pathways

Tap or hover to reveal

Clear residency pathways

The MM2H program offers Silver, Gold and Platinum tiers plus the SEZ option, giving Australians long term residency with a defined, documented process.

HEALTHCARE

World class, without the wait

Tap or hover to reveal

World class, without the wait

Malaysian private hospitals serve international patients daily. Private healthcare in Malaysia can be substantially cheaper than Australian private care, depending on procedure and provider.

LOCATION

Asia on your doorstep

Tap or hover to reveal

Asia on your doorstep

Malaysia makes regional Asian travel more accessible, with frequent low-cost routes available. Bali, Tokyo, Bangkok and Singapore all sit within easy reach.

COMMUNITY

You will not be doing it alone

Penang and KL host established Australian and international expat communities, international schools and familiar comforts when you want them.
